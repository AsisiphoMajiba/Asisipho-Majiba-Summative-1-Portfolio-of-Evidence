Our Story

From a small setting to King William's Town's premier salon experience.

Established 2018

We are a small business salon in King William's Town. This Beauty Salon started in 2018 in a small setting and it grew big as the time went by. Over the years we were able to secure our own place so that it can be easier for customers to find the location.

The Turning Point

When the Covid-19 hit, the business suffered a loss because of lack of interaction. We decided to introduce a new system of booking an appointment online so that we can limit the number of people inside the salon.

Present Day & Beyond

This system helped the business a lot, even when the Covid-19 ended we kept on receiving bookings online which grew the business even more!
